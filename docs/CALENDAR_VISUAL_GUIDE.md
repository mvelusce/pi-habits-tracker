## Usage Tips

1. **Quick Check-Ins**
   - Click today's date
   - Toggle your completed factors
   - Close and you're done!

2. **Review Past Days**
   - Navigate to previous months
   - Update any missed entries
   - See your historical patterns

3. **Focus on Categories**
   - Use filter to view just "Fitness" factors
   - Or just "Nutrition" factors
   - Helps when you have many factors

4. **Track Patterns**
   - Look for green streaks (consistent completion)
   - Identify problem days (lots of red)
   - Adjust your routine accordingly

## Pro Tips

- **Early in the day**: Calendar shows your progress so far
- **End of day**: Review and complete any remaining factors
- **Weekly review**: Look back at the week to spot trends
- **Monthly planning**: Navigate ahead to plan upcoming goals

## Accessibility

- Clear color contrast for all users
- Emojis provide additional visual cues
- Numbers show exact completion counts
- Large touch targets for mobile
- Keyboard navigable (Tab, Enter, Esc)
